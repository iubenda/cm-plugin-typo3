Welcome to Consent Manager

Follow the below steps to set up your Consent Manager.

  • First get your CMP Code-ID. Here is the link to register https://www.consentmanager.net/. We will provide you CMP Code-ID after registration.
  • Enter your CMP Code-ID in the Consent Manager settings page (Click the get started button below to access the settings page)
  • Choose your blocking mode (Automatic / Semi-Automatic)
  • Enter Custom HTML code (optional)
  • Click Submit button
